PagePal

Check it out here!
Created for the Women Who Code Hackathon for Social Good 2023.

A web app designed to enhance users' reading comprehension skills by offering a range of features. It provides access to a selection of public domain books for reading, along with the option to enable text-to-speech for auditory learning. The app employs AI to generate definitions of unfamiliar words within the text and offers AI-generated pop quizzes to test users' comprehension. Additionally, users can import their own texts, such as articles and textbook passages, to apply these features to their personal reading materials. PagePal offers a versatile and personalized learning experience to help users improve their reading comprehension.
